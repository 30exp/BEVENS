MANGO

  • The Flavor

    Sweet. Juicy. Tropical. Our Great Great Grandmothers love for
    mangoes was endless, She only loved just the right ones.

  • The Recipe

    2 ½ cups (280 grams) finely ground graham-cracker crumbs
    ⅓ cup plus 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
    ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
    1 large pinch sea salt
    9 tablespoons (128 grams) unsalted butter, melted
    ¾ cup cold water
    ½ cup granulated sugar
    2 tablespoons plus 1/4 teaspoon powdered gelatin (2 1/2 packages at 2 1/2 teaspoons per pack)
    ½ cup heavy whipping cream, chilled
    4 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
    1 30-ounce can Alphonso mango purée (3 1/4 cups)
    1 large pinch sea salt

  • The Source

    All of our Mangoes come from local farmers of the
    Masschuessets area.

    Everything we source is grown and raised
    organically and ethically.

KIWI

  • The Flavor

    the perfect bland of Kiwi's, Coconut, and just a hint of
    lime to remind one of the old times of past, and of the
    flavors of history.

  • The Recipe

    15 whole Graham Crackers (the 4-section Large Pieces)
    1/2 c. Macadamia Nuts
    1/3 c. Sugar
    1/3 c. Butter, Melted
    1 can Sweetened, Condensed Milk
    2 whole Egg Yolks
    1/2 c. Lime Juice
    1 tbsp. Lime Zest
    6 whole Kiwi, Peeled And Sliced
    Sweetened Whipped Cream For Serving
    Extra Kiwi Slices, For Serving
    Extra Chopped Macadamia Nuts, For Serving

  • The Source

    All of our kiwis come from local farmers of the
    Pennsylvania and New Hampshire areas.

    Everything we source is grown and raised
    organically and ethically.

EGG

  • The Flavor

    An egg tart in the Portuguese style. taken from the island
    traditions of the Azores. The creamy and rich flavor has made
    this a quick fan favorite in New England.

  • The Recipe

    1½ sticks unsalted butter, softened
    1¾ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
    ⅔ cup water
    ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
    1 cup granulated sugar
    ⅔ cup water
    1 cinnamon stick
    1 cup, plus 6
    ½ cup all-purpose flour
    6 egg yolks
    Ground cinnamon

  • The Source

    All of our eggs come from Pete & Gerry's Organic Eggs
    a farm in the New Hampshire area.

    Everything we source is grown and raised
    organically and ethically.
